- What you see is all there is.”
- **WYSIATI**
- Phenomenon of how people jump to conclusions on the basis of limited information. 
- Focus on existing evidence and ignore absent evidence.
- As a result, System 1 offers believable story based on limited evidence, and these impressions or intuitions can then be endorsed by System 2 and turn into deep-rooted values and *beliefs*.(LOL!)
