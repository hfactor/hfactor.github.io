[[Design Sprints]] follows unpack > ideate > evaluate > test > refine pattern while [[Lean UX]] follows a build > test > iterate loop. Design Sprint is more about evaluation, while lean UX is about evolution.

*At times, there is a problem with lean UX that user's are not very forgiving.* Not all business can attempt Lean UX, as it's hard to gain back the trust once it's lost. 

Design sprint is providing a user-validated draft, that provides a clarity on backlog for design and development.
