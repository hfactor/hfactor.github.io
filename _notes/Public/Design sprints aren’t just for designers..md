title: Design sprints aren’t just for designers.
notetype : lit
date: 2021-02-05
---

[[Design Sprints]] is a problem solving process that provides validation to solution. The focus is on identifying and understand problem and developing solution, and not design. **Here, design is just the medium of communication.** 

The result will be successful when cross-functional teams work together to uncover and test a problem or set of problems.
