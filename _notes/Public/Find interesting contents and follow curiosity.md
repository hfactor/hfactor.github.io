- Living your brain wandering and assuming that it will come with new subjects or ideas is time consuming process. 
- Instead of that, it is better to start from an interesting knowledge you have in your second brain, see the potential, ask questions, go through connected notes and start answering questions. 
- Just follow interest, curiosity and questions. Let curiosity lead the wander.
